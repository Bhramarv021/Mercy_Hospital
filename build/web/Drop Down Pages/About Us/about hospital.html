ABOUT HOSPITAL

INTRODUCTION

Mercy Healthcare Hospital is a widely recognised healthcare unit in India that offers new hope and solution to people seeking our care. We are dedicated to improve the quality of life of our patients by delivering advanced and compassionate care to all communities that we serve. We are committed to provide world class healthcare at affordable cost and have achieved success in touching the lives of many patients worldwide. Mercy Healthcare is a 242 bedded ,multidisciplinary hospital that has the best pool of highly qualified and experienced doctors to provide consultation as well as treatment to patients at all times, backed by the most efficient nurses and paramedics and managerial support staff. Our experts offer holistic team based care in promoting innovative and unsurpassed well-being of our patients while setting new benchmarks in medical & service excellence. With over 100 hospital based senior and junior consultants, we always have specialised healthcare providers to serve you in times of emergencies. We leave no stones unturned in pushing the boundaries to educate our next generation care providers as we regularly conduct special training sessions for enhancement of our services. Our instinctive devotion to enhance the wellness of the community motivates us to conduct Continuous Medical Education programs along with workshops and seminars; many of these are attended by doctors from different parts of India & abroad. The hospital is recognized by the National Boards of Examination (India) to impart post graduate training and teaching to DNB students. Therefore doctors are updated on recent trends. We offer full-fledged treatment services that range from primary care to complicated & rare disorders. We regularly keep upgrading our hospital with latest technology by acquiring new diagnostic and therapeutic equipments for early diagnosis and treatment. Our specialty services delivered with quality, compassion and respect have enabled us to achieve excellence in patients clinical outcome. We have successfully created our own niche across the healthcare spectrum. We aim to deliver right care at right time through right hands !!!

OBJECTIVE

To bring together in one place educational facilities of the highest order for the training of the personnel in all important branches of the health activity. To attain self sufficiency in postgraduate in medical education.

FUNCTIONS OF MERCY

Undergraduate and postgraduate teaching in medical and related physical biological sciences. Nursing and dental education Innovations in education. Producing medical teachers for the country. Research in medical and related sciences. Health care : preventive, promotive and curative; primary, secondary & tertiary. Community based teaching and research
