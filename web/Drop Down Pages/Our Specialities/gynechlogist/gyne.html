Obstetrics & Gynaecology

Cardio Thoracic Surgery “Transforming Women’s Healthcare with Compassion” The Institute of Obstetrics & Gynaecology at Mercy Hospital is a full-fledged “Women’s Health Unit” that caters to the healthcare needs of women of all ages starting from their adolescence, reproductive years, menopause and beyond. The department is headed by an expert Obstetrician/Gynaecologist having 35 years of experience in this speciality. We offer a comprehensive range of both inpatient and outpatient services that fall within the field of women’s general gynaecologic health as well as obstetric conditions. Our multidisciplinary team offers coordinated and supportive care for various gynaecological conditions like uterine fibroids, urinary incontinence, abnormal bleeding, chronic pelvic pain, pelvic floor disorders, gynaecologic cancers and menopause. The Institute also delivers personalised, comprehensive and quality care of highest standards to ensure you have a safe and healthy pregnancy as well as delivery. We offer most advanced gynaecologic and obstetric care to meet the individual needs of patients that include routine to high-risk pregnancies & deliveries. We also specialise in lifesaving prenatal treatment for fetel disease, utero procedures and infertility treatments. Our hospital is equipped with latest technology and state-of-the-art equipment to handle any kind of emergency situation. The obstetrical specialists have in-depth expertise to provide exceptional care before birth, during labor and after delivery. We help mothers-to-be in maintaining and improving their health by conducting regular prenatal examinations along with sophisticated screening & diagnostic procedures to check baby’s growth & development. We regularly monitor mother’s health as well so that any maternal complication can be diagnosed and treated on time. We help to shape the future of women’s health care and try to make your experience as healthy, enjoyable and memorable as possible.
