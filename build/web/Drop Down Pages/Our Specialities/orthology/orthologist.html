Orthopaedics

“Stronger Bones- The Stronger & Healthier You" Many people with knee, hip, back or other joint pain don’t undergo treatment because of the fear of surgery. At Jaipur Golden Hospital, we follow personalised and patient-centric approach to make the patients more cared for and comfortable. We completely understand the challenges faced by the patients during the difficult period of treatment & recovery. Our highly skilled Senior Orthopaedic Consultant(D Ortho, MS, FIAMS) with 35 years of experience in this field guides the patients through the entire process and offers one-stop solution to meet the needs of the patient with additional peace of mind. Our orthopaedic surgeons are recognized nationally as well as internationally as leaders for using minimally invasive techniques and bone conserving techniques which is of great benefit to the patients resulting in less blood loss, reduced pain, faster recovery and shorter stay at the hospital. We relentlessly strive to improve the quality of life for our patients and support them at every step of the way.
