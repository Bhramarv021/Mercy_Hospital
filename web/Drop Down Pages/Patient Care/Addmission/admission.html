ADMISSION PROCESS

  • Patient arrives at admission desk with request of admission advised by treating consultant/Chief Medical Officer (CMO)
  • Front office executive checks whether the patient belongs to any Third Party Administrators (TPA) / Panel
  • Patient is admitted subject to the availability of desired bed category
  • Front office staff will give estimate according to relevant bed category or Surgery advised
  • Patient needs to deposit advance payment as per estimate given
  • The advance shall be adjusted against the final bill at the time of discharge
  • The staff escorts the patient to the allotted room/bed.
  • Documents required at the time of Admission:-
  • ID proof of patient
  • Pan card of patient (In case of minor patient, Pan card of Parents is required)
  • Registration number, if already issued
  • Records of previous investigation and prescription
  • TPA/Panel card with dependant proof
